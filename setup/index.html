Almost there — next steps

  1. Fork the template repo: github.com/NikoServes/nikoserves-linkinbio-template
  2. Save the downloaded config.json at the root of your fork (replacing the existing one).
  3. Replace /images/avatar-placeholder.svg with your photo (and /images/avatar.png for the favicon).
  4. Commit and push.
  5. Connect a Cloudflare Pages project to your fork (framework: None, build command: empty, output: /).
  6. Add your custom domain via Pages → Settings → Custom domains.
